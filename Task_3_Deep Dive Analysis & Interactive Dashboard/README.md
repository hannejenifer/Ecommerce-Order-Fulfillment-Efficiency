# Task 3 – Deep Dive Analysis & Interactive Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project focuses on performing deep-dive business analysis on the Olist E-Commerce dataset and developing an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze sales performance, customer distribution, seller performance, and order delivery efficiency.

The objective of this task is to move beyond basic visualization and identify meaningful business patterns through KPI tracking and multi-dimensional analysis.

## Objective

The main objectives of this task were:

• Define core business KPIs  
• Perform deep-dive analysis on sales and order performance  
• Analyze customer and seller distribution  
• Evaluate delivery efficiency  
• Build an interactive dashboard for business decision support  

## Tools Used

Power BI – Interactive dashboard development  
Python (Google Colab) – Supporting analysis for deep insights  
Data Analytics techniques – KPI analysis & trend analysis  

## KPIs Used

The dashboard tracks key business performance indicators:

**Total Revenue (~$16M)**  - 
Measures overall business performance.

**Total Orders (~99K)**  - 
Indicates transaction volume.

**Average Order Value (~$161)**  - 
Measures customer spending behavior.

**Average Delivery Time (~12 days)**  - 
Measures logistics efficiency.

**On-time Delivery Rate (~92%)**  - 
Measures operational performance.

## Deep Dive Analysis Areas

The deep analysis focused on three major business dimensions:

### 1. Sales Performance Analysis

Analysis included:

• Monthly revenue trends  
• Top revenue generating product categories  
• Revenue distribution across states  
• Order growth trends  

### 2. Customer & Seller Analysis

Analysis included:

• Customer distribution by region  
• Seller concentration by state  
• Revenue contribution by sellers  
• Seller performance segmentation  

### 3. Order Performance Analysis

Analysis included:

• Delivery time distribution  
• Late delivery patterns  
• On-time vs delayed delivery comparison  
• Payment method contribution  

## Key Business Insights

From the deep-dive analysis, the following insights were identified:

1. The platform generated approximately $16M revenue from about 99K orders, showing strong transaction volume.

2. A few product categories contribute disproportionately to revenue, indicating category concentration risk.

3. São Paulo dominates both customer base and revenue contribution, showing geographic dependency.

4. Seller performance distribution shows most sellers operate at lower revenue tiers, suggesting opportunity for seller development strategies.

5. Delivery performance is strong with high on-time delivery rates, indicating operational stability.

6. Credit card payments dominate transactions, showing strong digital payment adoption.

7. Late deliveries are concentrated in specific regions, indicating logistics optimization opportunities.

## Dashboard Capabilities

The dashboard allows users to:

• Monitor KPIs dynamically  
• Analyze revenue trends  
• Compare regional performance  
• Evaluate seller contributions  
• Track delivery efficiency  
• Filter by category, state and year  

## Business Conclusion

The analysis shows the e-commerce platform demonstrates strong revenue generation and operational efficiency. However, revenue concentration in specific categories and geographic regions indicates potential business risk.

Improvement opportunities include:

• Expanding high performing product categories  
• Improving low performing seller segments  
• Optimizing logistics in late delivery regions  
• Diversifying geographic revenue sources  

The dashboard provides a decision-support tool for identifying growth opportunities and operational improvements.
